120-cell-adventure

A 600-maze platform adventure on the graph of the 120-cell.

Gold0/0

Keyboard: A/D or left/right arrows move; W, Space, or Z jumps; one airborne double jump is available before landing; X or clicking in the maze defends using stored gold. Mobile: drag the left thumbstick to move, then use Jump and Defend. Map: M toggles full map; V cycles all/discovered/undiscovered; C cycles focus modes. Drag maps to rotate or pan; wheel or pinch to zoom.
