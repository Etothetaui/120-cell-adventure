120-cell-adventure

A 600-maze platform adventure on the graph of the 120-cell.

Keyboard: A/D or left/right arrows move; W, Space, or Z jumps; tap for a short hop or hold for full height; one airborne double jump is available before landing; X or clicking in the maze defends using stored energy once energy capacity is unlocked; K kills/respawns the player; Esc pauses/resumes. Mobile: use Direction controls to cycle joystick, buttons, or tilt movement; then tap/hold Jump and use Defend once energy capacity is unlocked. Map: M toggles full map; V cycles all/discovered/undiscovered; C cycles focus modes. Drag maps to rotate or pan; wheel or pinch to zoom.
